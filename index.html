Time

Once upon a time...


You are the witches' servant. She is in a hurry to attend a last minute secret gathering of the witches in the Forest of the Haunted. In a rush, she yells at you:


"Quick, gather my belongings! What are you waiting for?? DO I NEED TO TEACH YOU A LESSON!??..."






It could have been worse


Phew, lucky you! You managed to collect the witches' belongings in a hectic rush and handed them over to her with a submissive gesture. A seigh escapes her lips, then she says:


"Servant, I will be gone for the next 6 long days. You will have the honor to perform a thorough spring cleaning of the house. I will leave Artemis here to observe you and to give me daily reports of your progress and whereabouts. Until then..."

Game Over

"Oh, you still aren't done yet??! I knew from the beginning that lazy ass of yours was useless! Let's see if you suffice as a vessel to try my newly aqcuired incense instead..."


...and with one quick witches' magic wand movement you suddenly vanish into a dusty haze of nothingness which after a few moments unveils a peculiar carved pumpkin that now serves as decoration in the witches house

...until it rots away.
